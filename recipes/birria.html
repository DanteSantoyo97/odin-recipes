Birria

Description

Birria is a meat stew or soup, mainly made with goat or beef.

Ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • garlic
  • onion
  • beef chuck roast
  • dried chiles
  • cinnamon

Steps

  1. Fry the chiles:In a medium sauce pan, add all the dried chiles, tomatoes, onion, garlic, cinnamon till they get a char
  2. Blend:Once the chiles have a char, transfer everything to a blender and add 1 cup of water, blend until smooth.
  3. Sear the meat and cook:Once the meat is seared, pour the blended chiles in to the pan with the seared meat, stiring. Bring it to a boil, then turn the heat to low, cover, and cook for 4 hours until meat is tender enough to fall apart.
