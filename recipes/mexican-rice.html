Mexican Rice

Description

Mexican rice is a mexican dish comprised of rice, tomato sauce and chile spices

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice
  • One Tomatoe
  • One Onion
  • Two Seranos
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put two cups of jasmine rice into pan and saute it till browned with some oil in the bottom of the pan.
  2. Blend tomatoes and seranos and poor it into the sauce pan with the rice.
  3. Cut up the onion and put it in with the rice.
  4. Salt the sauce pan and then bring the pan to a boil.
  5. Once the sauce pan is brought to a boil, cover the rice and lower the heat to medium heat for 15 minutes.
  6. Stir the rice and then lower the head to low and cover for 15 more minutes.
