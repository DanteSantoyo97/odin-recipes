Beef Stew

Description

Beef stew is a soup comprised of potatoes, beef and tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Seranos

Steps

  1. Cut up the onion and seranos into a pan with oil and saute.
  2. Cut up the meat and put it into the pan with the sauted onion and seranos.
  3. Dice the tomatoes and potatoes and put them in the pan once the meat is cooked.
  4. Bring the tomato soup to a boil, once its at a boil, turn the heat to low and let the potatoes cook through until desired texture
